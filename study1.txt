Dataset title
Investigating neuroepigenetic alterations in chronic low back pain with positron emission tomography

Description
This dataset contains multimodal neuroimaging data acquired from patients with chronic low back pain. Data include positron emission tomography (PET) with the radiotracer [11C]Martinostat, structural MRI (T1-weighted, T2-weighted, and FLAIR), and resting-state functional MRI.

Participants
All participants were patients diagnosed with chronic low back pain. No healthy control subjects are included in this dataset.

Imaging modalities
PET: [11C]Martinostat
Structural MRI: T1w, T2w, FLAIR
Functional MRI: resting-state BOLD
De-identification
All structural MRI images (T1w, T2w, and FLAIR) were defaced prior to public release to remove facial features and protect participant privacy. Functional MRI and PET images are provided as brain images and do not contain identifiable facial features.

Study design
This is an observational, cross-sectional study. No experimental intervention or task was performed during image acquisition.

Funding
This work was supported by the National Institute of Arthritis and Musculoskeletal and Skin Diseases (NIAMS), National Institutes of Health, under Cooperative Agreement UH3AR076741.

Notes
The dataset is organized according to the Brain Imaging Data Structure (BIDS) specification.
